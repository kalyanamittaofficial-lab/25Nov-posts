The Four Factors of Stream-Entry

We all wish to walk the path to Nibbana. To reach the first stage, Sotapatti (Stream-entry), four factors must be fulfilled. The path is as follows:

(We hope to bring a more detailed article on the Factors of Stream-Entry soon.)

1. Association with Good Friends
+

As even King Pasenadi of Kosala understood, this noble Dhamma taught by the Blessed One is for one who has good friends, not for one who has bad friends.

According to the Sigalovada Sutta, there are four types of true good friends:

1. The Helper

  • Protects the friend when they are heedless.
  • Protects the friend's property.
  • Is a refuge in times of danger.
  • Provides help (double the amount) when needed.

2. The Friend in Happiness and Sorrow

  • Tells their secrets to the friend.
  • Guards the friend's secrets.
  • Does not abandon the friend in misfortune.
  • Is willing to sacrifice even their life for the friend.

3. The Mentor (Who Points Out What is Beneficial)

  • Restrains the friend from evil.
  • Establishes them in good.
  • Teaches them what they do not know.
  • Shows them the path to a good destination.

4. The Compassionate Friend

  • Grieves at the friend's decline.
  • Rejoices in their progress.
  • Prevents others from speaking ill of the friend.
  • Praises those who speak well of them.
